About Sean 👋

The man behind the castles. A bit about who you are booking with and why families keep coming back.

Bouncy Castles R U

Bouncy Castles R U is run by Sean Neill, an Irish bouncy castle hire operator who has built a reputation on one simple principle: show up on time, set up properly, and make the kids smile.

Sean started out with a single castle and a van. The fleet has grown since then but the approach has not changed. Every booking gets the same attention, every castle is cleaned between hires, and every customer gets Sean's personal number so they can reach him directly, any time.

Whether it is a backyard birthday party for ten kids or a school fete with hundreds running around, Sean treats every event the same. The details matter.
